## 1. Product Vision & Release Strategy

NexaPOS ERP aims to bridge the digital gap for Indonesian businesses by delivering an integrated, high-performance platform. The release strategy prioritizes a stable core commerce engine, followed by financial and operational extensions, and finally ecosystem integrations.

---

## 2. Roadmap Phases Overview

### Phase 1: Foundation & Core Commercial POS (Month 1 - 4)
*Goal: Launch the multi-tenant core framework, inventory foundations, and POS terminal interface.*

- **Onboarding and Multi-Tenancy:**
  - Multi-tenant sub-domain database routing and account registration.
  - Granular Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) security matrix settings.
- **Inventory and Catalog:**
  - Multi-branch and warehouse directory configurations.
  - Master products catalog supporting SKU variations and barcodes.
- **POS Operations:**
  - Fast touch POS register screen with barcode scanning.
  - Offline POS data operations using local storage database sync.
  - Cash and basic QRIS integrations.

---

### Phase 2: Supply Chain, Accounting, & HR Core (Month 5 - 8)
*Goal: Deliver back-office ERP features including supply chain procurement, double-entry bookkeeping, and staff management.*

- **Supply Chain:**
  - Purchase Orders workflow, approvals, and Goods Receiving worksheet.
  - Stock Opname audits with manager approval limits.
- **Accounting & Tax:**
  - Double-entry Chart of Accounts (COA) configurations.
  - Auto-generated journal postings from sales and procurement transactions.
  - Real-time General Ledger, P&L, and Balance Sheet generation.
  - Local Indonesian tax calculation rules (PPN/PPh).
- **Human Resources:**
  - Employee profiles and shifts roster scheduling.
  - Geo-Fenced clock-in/out attendance logging.

---

### Phase 3: Marketing Automation & Channel Integrations (Month 9 - 12)
*Goal: Drive growth with marketing tools, customer loyalty engines, payment gateway integrations, and API accessibility.*

- **Loyalty Program:**
  - CRM profiles with tiered loyalty membership points multiplier settings.
  - Dynamic promotional rules and vouchers validation.
- **Notification Services:**
  - WhatsApp Business API notification alerts for orders and receipts.
  - Real-time in-app, SMS, and Email update dispatchers.
- **Integrations:**
  - Payment gateway webhooks for automatic QRIS settlement.
  - Split and digital payment options (GoPay, OVO, DANA).
- **Open Access:**
  - Developer portal, REST API keys, and rate-limiting configs.

---

### Phase 4: Omnichannel Sync & Enterprise Expansion (Month 13 - 18)
*Goal: Expand market footprint with omnichannel marketplace integrations, native mobile applications, and AI operations.*

- **Omnichannel Sync:**
  - E-Commerce storefront integrations (Tokopedia, Shopee).
  - Centralized inventory sync across online and offline channels.
- **Mobile Applications:**
  - Native iOS and Android application packages.
- **AI Operations:**
  - Machine learning demand forecasting for stock reordering.
  - Automated ledger transaction classification.
- **Enterprise Features:**
  - Multi-company consolidation accounts.
  - Custom report builder interface.
